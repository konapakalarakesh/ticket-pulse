ℹ️ How It Works
  • Auth: Uses your browser's existing Midway session — no passwords stored.
  • Ticket Check: Fetches t.corp ticket page using your credentials (same-session fetch).
  • Audit Data: Pulls your .xlsx from GitHub (same-origin or raw link).
  • Hosting: Pure static HTML — push to GitHub Pages and share with your team.
  • Cache: Status results are cached in localStorage for 30 min to avoid re-fetching.
